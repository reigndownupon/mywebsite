Jesus-Daily

Daily Scripture snippets for a Jesus-centered life.

These short reflections come from the daily Jesus-Daily rhythm: Scripture first, Jesus at the center, and practical questions that invite honest obedience without shame or hype.

June 13, 2026 · Micah 6:6–8

Daily Christ-orientation check

Micah calls God’s people beyond religious performance into a life marked by justice, mercy, and humble walking with God. In Jesus, we see this fully embodied: truth without pride, mercy without compromise, and obedience rooted in love for the Father and neighbor.

  1. Where today might Jesus be calling me to choose quiet faithfulness over visible religious effort or self-proving?
  2. Who needs me to practice justice, mercy, or humility in a concrete way before the day is over?

Lord Jesus, form in me a humble obedience that looks like Your mercy and truth.

June 12, 2026 · Isaiah 58:6–9

Mercy that makes worship honest

Jesus teaches us that devotion to God becomes visible in mercy, justice, generosity, and love for people who are burdened or overlooked.
